Yugoslavia: The Avoidable War (2002)


YUGOSLAVIA: THE AVOI...
OVERVIEW
SYNOPSIS
The documentary which took four years to produce, and which was updated following NATO intervention in Kosovo, investigates how serious errors and misjudgments made by Western powers, particularly Germany and the United States helped spark the violent break-up of the former Yugoslavia in 1991 and continue to destabilize the region in the new millennium. more...
